Applying for a car loan

We know how big a deal buying a car can be — whether it’s your first set of wheels, an upgrade for the family, or something with a bit more grunt for work. At Londy, we cut through the noise to help you get the right loan without the headaches.

Forget hours of chasing banks or decoding loan terms. We compare offers from our panel of lenders, handle the paperwork, and walk you through it all — step by step — so you can focus on the fun part: getting the car.



How it usually works with us

You tell us what kind of car you’ve got your eye on (new or used, dealer or private), and we’ll start lining up the right options behind the scenes. We’ll show you what’s out there — what it’ll cost, what the repayments look like — and answer the stuff Google can’t.

Once you’re happy with the numbers, we handle the application and work with the lender to get it across the line quickly. Most clients are approved within a day or two.



Finance that fits your drive

We’re not here to sell you on a one-size-fits-all loan. We’re here to help you move forward — with car finance that actually fits your budget, your timeline, and your life.
